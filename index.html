Gyre Immigration

Gyre Immigration is based on the east coast of Canada in Halifax, Nova Scotia. We offer immigration and relocation consulting services for people looking to move to Canada. Our services will guide you through the process of immigrating to Canada on a temporary or permanent basis. For employers, we design and help manage the recruitment and settlement of foreign nationals.

We specialize in offing the following services:

Temporary Resident Applications

- Labour Market Impact Assessments (for employers)
- Work Permits (and renewals)

Permanent Resident Applications

- Nova Scotia Provincial Nominee Programs
- Atlantic Immigration Program
- Family Sponsorship
- Express Entry
- Family Sponsorship

Other

- Permanent Resident Card Applications Renewals (and questionnaires)
- Citizenship Applications (and questionnaires)

As a member in good standing with the Immigration Consultants of Canada Regulatory Council, the team at Gyre Immigration are permitted to act on your behalf in matters before Citizenship and Immigration Canada (CIC) and the Canada Border Services Agency (CBSA).
